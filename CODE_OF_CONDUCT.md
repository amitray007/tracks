# Code of conduct

## Our standard

Contributors and maintainers must help keep Tracks welcoming, constructive, and safe. Be respectful, critique ideas and code rather than people, assume good intent while addressing impact, and respect privacy boundaries.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, publishing another person's private information, or deliberately exposing private session data or credentials.

## Scope

This standard applies in the repository and in project-related public or private communication.

## Reporting and enforcement

For non-sensitive concerns, open an issue requesting maintainer attention without repeating harmful or private material. For reports involving private information, credentials, or a security concern, use the private process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior threatens the project or its community. Reports will be reviewed as promptly and confidentially as the available channel permits.
