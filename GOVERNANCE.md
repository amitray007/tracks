# Governance

Tracks currently uses a maintainer-led model while the architecture and security boundaries are still pre-release.

## Decisions

- Small fixes and implementation details are decided through pull-request review.
- Product, protocol, storage, authentication, or provider-model changes should begin with an issue or design proposal.
- Durable architectural decisions are recorded in `docs/architecture/decisions`.
- Privacy and security constraints take precedence over convenience or compatibility.

Maintainers make final decisions after considering evidence, compatibility, maintenance cost, and community feedback. Decisions should be explained in the relevant issue, pull request, or architecture record.

## Contributions and maintainership

Contributions are welcomed under [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md). Consistent contributors may be invited to take on additional review or maintenance responsibilities based on demonstrated judgment, reliability, and respect for the project's privacy rules.

The governance model may evolve as the contributor community grows. Material changes to it will be reviewed publicly in the repository.
